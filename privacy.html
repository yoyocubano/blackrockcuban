Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026 · Effective immediately

1. Who We Are

BlackRockCuban ("we", "our", "us") is operated by Welux Events Holding under the domain blackrockcuban.weluxevents.store. Contact: yucolaguilar@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

  • Purchase data: name, email, shipping address — collected via Stripe at checkout
  • Payment data: processed exclusively by Stripe Inc. We never store card numbers
  • Order fulfillment data: shared with Gelato (white-label apparel) and Printful to ship your order
  • Analytics: anonymous page views via Cloudflare Web Analytics (no cookies)
  • Cookie consent: stored in localStorage on your device

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Contract performance: processing orders and shipping
  • Legitimate interest: fraud prevention, security
  • Consent: non-essential cookies (you may withdraw anytime)

5. Data Retention

Order data is retained for 7 years (legal/tax obligation). You may request deletion of non-mandatory data at any time.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights: yucolaguilar@gmail.com

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies (checkout session, consent preference). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can clear cookies at any time from your browser settings.

8. Data Security

  • HTTPS/TLS enforced on all pages
  • Security headers: X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, CSP
  • Rate limiting on all API endpoints
  • No card data stored on our servers (Stripe handles all PCI-DSS compliance)
